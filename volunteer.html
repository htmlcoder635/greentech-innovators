Volunteer With Us

Lend your skills to combat climate change. We're looking for passionate individuals in a variety of roles—all remote and open-source.

Current Roles & Needs

Software Engineer (Python/JS)

Help build and maintain our core analysis tools and backend infrastructure. Experience with data processing libraries is a plus.

Data Scientist / Analyst

Contribute to statistical modeling, data cleaning, and interpreting environmental datasets for our public reports.

Technical Writer / Editor

Create clear documentation, project guides, and impactful reports summarizing our findings for a general audience.

Citizen Scientist Coordinator

Help organize and manage remote data collection efforts and manage volunteer teams around specific ecological projects.

UX/UI Designer (Figma/Web)

Improve the usability and visual design of our web tools and mobile applications to make science more accessible.

How It Works

  • **Find Your Fit:** Review the roles above and find where your passion and skills align with our projects.
