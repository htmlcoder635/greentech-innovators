About Us

Empowering students worldwide to use AI for climate impact.

Who We Are

GreenTech Innovators is a student-led global initiative dedicated to building accessible, impactful AI tools for understanding and addressing climate change. Our mission is to empower students, educators, and communities around the world to use technology for meaningful environmental action.

What began as a passion project has grown into a collaborative climate technology hub — where anyone can explore data, run AI-powered climate models, and contribute to open-source climate solutions.
